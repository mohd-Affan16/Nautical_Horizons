Nautical Horizons

Nautical Horizons Cruises is a premier ship traveling company designed to offer passengers a luxurious and adventurous ocean voyage. With a focus on blending relaxation with exploration, the company provides an unforgettable experience featuring breathtaking ocean views, world-class onboard entertainment, and gourmet dining options. The flagship vessel, the Horizon Explorer, ensures a comfortable and exciting journey for all travelers, making it an ideal choice for a memorable getaway.

Sailing Route and Destinations

The cruise embarks from the vibrant Port of Miami in Florida, USA, where passengers board amidst a lively coastal atmosphere, ready to set sail across the Atlantic. The final destination is the stunning Nassau Harbor in the Bahamas, a tropical paradise renowned for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant cultural heritage. In Nassau, passengers can enjoy shore excursions such as snorkeling, visiting historic forts, or relaxing on the beach, making it a perfect stop for adventure and leisure.

Timings and Schedule

Sailings are scheduled for a convenient weekend getaway, departing from Miami every Friday at 5:00 PM and arriving in Nassau by 10:00 AM on Saturday. The return journey leaves Nassau on Sunday at 4:00 PM, docking back in Miami by 9:00 AM on Monday. This schedule allows passengers to maximize their time exploring Nassau while enjoying a full weekend of cruising and onboard activities.
